Visit Bermuda!

Bermuda is a beautiful Territory in the North Atlantic Ocean. There are 181 islands which make up Bermuda, many of which are connected by bridge. It is known for its beaches and clear blue water. The weather is usually nice and sunny.

There are about 63,000 residents in Bermuda. According to UNWTO, 85,000 tourists visited in 2021, and the the tourism industry contributed 2.2% of the total GDP in that same year. Cruise guests are a large portion of the tourists. There are many ways to get around the islands, including taking the bus, cycling, taking a ferry, taxis, electric rental cars, and more. Renting a boat is something else people like to do while in Bermuda.
